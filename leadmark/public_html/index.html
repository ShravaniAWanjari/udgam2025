Welcome to Udhgam, the flagship innovation and technology festival of Woxsen University

Join us in celebrating the synergy of technology, creativity, and ingenuity. Sponsoring Udhgam offers you unparalleled access to a diverse audience of aspiring innovators, industry leaders, and young visionaries.

Udgham, Woxsen University's flagship innovation and technology festival, is set to take place from February 19th to 21st, 2025.

Over three days, this groundbreaking event will bring together aspiring innovators, industry leaders, entrepreneurs, and young visionaries to celebrate creativity, knowledge, and the transformative power of technology.

Udgham is designed around four dynamic segments, each offering a unique platform for students, professionals, and organizations to collaborate, compete, and create
